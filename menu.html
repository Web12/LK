1 Examples

This is the horizontal submenus example demo page.
Click the menu-button in the header to open the menu.

One of the menu-items has a submenu and a sub-submenu. All submenus will slide in from the right, pushing their parent item out to the left.

Note how the plugin automatically adds a back-button above each submenu.
